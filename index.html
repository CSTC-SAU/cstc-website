How We Operate.

CSTC functions like a real-world tech organization, divided into four core pillars.

Tech

Manages the logic and technical environments for all events. From building and maintaining platforms to propelling theoretical and practical technical education.

Design

The creative engine. Deals with all visual communications including UI/UX, event posters, certificates, club branding, and logos.

Public Relations

The voice of the club. Handles social media, promotions, and serves as the official channel between CSTC, external entities, and other organizations.

Operations

The backbone of our events. Deals with all logistical planning, financial management, and on-the-ground event execution.
