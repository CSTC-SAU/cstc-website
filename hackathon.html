Eligibility & Teams

  • Open to undergraduate students enrolled in recognized Indian institutions.
  • Teams must have exactly 3 to 5 members (Cross-college teams permitted).
  • Individual participation is not allowed. A participant can only join one team.
  • Valid institutional ID required. Team composition cannot be altered after Phase I.

Finale Logistics

  • Venue: South Asian University, New Delhi
  • Food: Meals provided during the 24H round.
  • Tech: Bring your own laptops/hardware. Internet is provided.
  • Note: Travel expenses are not reimbursed unless specified.

Final Evaluation Weightage

20% Innovation & Originality
20% Tech Architecture
20% Implementation
15% Problem Clarity
15% Scalability & Security
10% Demo & Presentation

Strict Rules

Violation leads to immediate disqualification.

• No plagiarism or copied implementations.

• External assistance during 24H is strictly prohibited.

• Pre-built templates allowed, but core logic must be built on-site.

• Missing submission deadlines results in rejection.
